# Jack Laughlin — Personal Landing Page

## 1. Overview
This project is a personal professional landing page for Jack Laughlin, a Business Analytics student at the University of Iowa. The site is designed to showcase his technical skills, leadership experience, and data-focused projects in a way that goes beyond a traditional resume. It serves as a centralized digital presence for recruiters, law school admissions, and professional connections. The goal is to communicate both analytical capability and long-term career direction toward law.

---

## 2. Purpose
The purpose of this project is to create a clean, accessible, and professional web presence that highlights Jack’s strengths as a data-driven problem solver and student-athlete. This landing page helps recruiters and admissions representatives quickly understand his background, projects, and career goals. It bridges the gap between technical ability and professional storytelling. The site is intended to generate interviews, outreach, and opportunities in both analytics and future legal pathways.

---

## 3. Target Audience
- Data internship recruiters  
- Law recruiters  
- Law school admissions representatives  
- Midwest-based companies and organizations  

---

## 4. Features

- Professional hero section with name and tagline  
- Clear introduction and personal bio  
- Organized skills section (Programming, Tools, Analytics)  
- Highlighted technical projects with outcomes  
- Clean and minimal user interface  
- Mobile-responsive design  
- Navigation bar with anchor links  
- Professional headshot display  
- Contact section with LinkedIn, GitHub, and email  
- Structured layout for readability
